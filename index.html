Dwelling in God’s presence is the key to Godly living.
Luke 10:41-42

  • Learn More

Women maintaining God’s presence in their lives

through Fellowship Reading the word Prayer Quiet time


It’s not enough to open your heart to Christ as Martha opened the door of her home to Jesus in salvation.
Staying constantly at His feet is in fact the one thing that is needful. Martha was distracted by the worldly things. Even thought they seem like they are important: cooking, education, marriage, getting your hair done, shopping for clothes for yourself and family, purchasing material things e.g. homes, cars, careers etc. They are nice to have but only one thing you need. So don’t replace what is needful with is nice to have. Listening to what Jesus has to say to you every moment of your life is more important than that movie that takes your whole day or that job that you bring home or rush off to early in the morning without first meditating on His word.

Luke 10:41-42, Martha Luke 10: 38-42, Psalm 91, Jeremiah 33:3

Standing in the gap in this time and age.
